Hi, I'm Aniello Napoleone!

I'm a 28 years old guy based in Ischia, Napoli. I'm a photographer wth a passion for street photography and reportage photography. I have mainly been concerned with bearing witness to the social movements that animate the city of Naples, where people fight for human rights, peace and equality. My goal is to broaden horizons, to continue to tell the battles of the marginalized, their lives, their demands, to demonstrate that in any part of the world, they are always the same. In every place on the planet the marginalized fight the same battle.
